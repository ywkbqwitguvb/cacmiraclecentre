Our History

  • 1985: CAC Miracle Centres founded as a small prayer fellowship.
  • 1995: Moved to current location, expanded to district headquarters.
  • 2005: Launched youth and outreach ministries.
  • 2015: Major building renovation and new worship center.
  • 2020: Online ministry and live streaming launched.

What We Believe

  • The Bible: God's inspired Word, our guide for faith and life.
  • Salvation: By grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • The Holy Spirit: Active in believers, empowering for service and spiritual growth.
  • Prayer: Central to our relationship with God and our ministry.
  • Community: We are called to love, serve, and support one another.
