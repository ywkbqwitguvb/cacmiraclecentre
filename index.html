About Us

We are the CAC Miracle Centres District Headquarters, a Christ-centered community devoted to prayer, worship, and service. Founded on faith and the power of the Holy Spirit, our mission is to raise transformative disciples who impact their community. Our vision is a thriving church where miracles, love, and unity abound.

Our History

Rooted in the apostolic revival, our district has grown into a strong hub for community and spiritual growth.

Mission

To preach Christ, build believers, and serve our community through Spirit-led ministry.

Vision

To be a beacon of hope where lives are transformed by the Word, prayer, and worship.
